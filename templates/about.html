Causes of disease in leaf of plant:

Plant leaf diseases are generally categorized into two types based on their cause: biotic (infectious) and abiotic (non-infectious) factors. Biotic diseases are caused by living pathogens such as fungi, bacteria, and viruses, which can spread from one plant to another. In contrast, abiotic disorders are caused by non-living environmental factors, such as nutrient deficiencies, extreme temperatures, or improper watering, and they are not transmissible between plants.
